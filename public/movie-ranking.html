Top 3 "Family Movie Night" Movies To Watch

Ranked Top To Bottom

Family Man

Heart warming story about a successful business man who unwillingly
gets the chance to see what his life would have been like had he chosen
love over success.

The Greatest Showman

A romanticized telling of the origins of the infamous Barnum Circus.
Fantastic musical numbers with outstanding choreography and singing
performances.

Moana

A very cute story about island cultural myths involving the adventure of a
young girl who partners with a demi-God to save her community.
Great song writing and performances, along with a beautifully created CGI
world, and an all around fun story.
